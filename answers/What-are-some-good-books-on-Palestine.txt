Recommended reading list:
Introduction
Works considered an excellent starting point to learn about Palestine. If you can only read one book on the subject, you can’t go wrong with any of these.
Said, Edward W. The question of Palestine. Vintage, 1992.
Khalidi, Rashid. The Hundred Years’ War on Palestine: A History of Settler Colonialism and Resistance, 1917–2017. Metropolitan Books, 2020.
Pappe, Ilan. The ethnic cleansing of Palestine. Simon and Schuster, 2007.
History
Palestinian history is long, multifaceted and often misunderstood. This is further complicated by the trend to neglect Palestinian history not directly tied to the modern question of Palestine. This selection of works will help you to better understand Palestinian history from a wider perspective. For the sake of simplicity, they have been divided into four broad categories.

One state
Has the two-state solution paradigm reached its end? Was it ever a truly viable option? These works discuss the reemergence of the one-state solution and what it could mean for the future.

The ‘peace’ process
